About Us

At ADYASAKTI, we are dedicated to empowering the most vulnerable sections of society by fostering independence and economic growth. Our mission is to support individuals with disabilities by helping them set up small businesses, equipping them with the necessary skills and resources to thrive independently. We also focus on empowering women by providing them with opportunities and support to start their own businesses, enhancing their economic stability and community involvement.

In addition to our economic development programs, we nurture a sense of community and cultural identity through our own cultural and sports teams. These initiatives offer a platform for personal development and social interaction, while promoting physical health and cultural expression among our members. Join us in making a significant impact in the lives of many and help us change the world one step at a time.
